About Us

Tired of playing the same game over and over because it never got a sequel? Don't worry, we have you covered. At Fake VALVE, we make games that does not exist, so that you and your friends don't have to wait an internity for a game sequel that's never going to arrive. We use state-of-the-art technology to pirate games from other companies and make them better. Recent works include Grand Theft Auto VI and Half Life 3 and we are taking suggestions from the community before our next project. Tell us what you want us to do next. Anything that hasn't seen a sequel in more than 5 years is fair game. Follow us on twitter to learn more.
